# Go-to-Market Strategy Summary

## Positioning

Gluten Zero is positioned as an affordable, locally produced gluten-free flour designed specifically for soft Pakistani roti.

## Core Message

**Soft Roti. Zero Gluten.**

## Target Segments

1. Celiac patients  
2. Gluten-sensitive consumers  
3. Health-conscious families  
4. Nutritionist-referred patients  

## Channels

| Channel | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Instagram/Facebook | Awareness, education, product demos |
| WhatsApp | Orders, support and repeat communication |
| Retail shelves | Physical visibility and local trust |
| Nutritionists/pharmacies | High-trust referrals |
| Bykea/InDrive | Same-day local delivery |

## Launch Activities

- Product education through roti-performance messaging
- Meta ads test for Lahore customers
- WhatsApp order handling
- Retail placement in Sheikhupura
- Nutritionist referral model

## Key Learning

For gluten-free products, customer trust is critical. Packaging, ingredient clarity, nutritionist support and real roti demonstration are more important than generic food branding.
