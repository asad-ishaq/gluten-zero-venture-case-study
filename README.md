# Gluten Zero by Aljannat Foods — Venture Case Study

## Project Overview

Gluten Zero by Aljannat Foods is a student-led entrepreneurship venture developed at Lahore Business School, University of Lahore. The venture focuses on a locally produced gluten-free multi-grain flour designed specifically for Pakistani households that need a soft roti alternative.

The product addresses a clear market gap: many gluten-free products are imported, expensive, baking-focused, or unsuitable for daily roti preparation.

## My Role

**Project Lead and Marketing Lead**

I led the venture direction, brand positioning, customer communication, digital campaign planning, go-to-market strategy, pitch preparation and coordination across team roles.

## Problem

Pakistani households rely heavily on wheat-based roti, but celiac and gluten-sensitive consumers cannot safely consume wheat. Existing alternatives are often expensive, imported, baking-focused, or poor in roti performance.

## Solution

Gluten Zero provides a gluten-free multi-grain flour positioned around:

- Roti Special Formula
- 1 kg resealable pouch
- Rs. 999 price point
- Gluten-free and wheat-free positioning
- Soft roti performance
- Nutritionist-supported credibility

## Target Market

- Celiac patients
- Gluten-sensitive consumers
- Health-conscious families
- Nutritionist-referred patients

## Business Model

The venture follows an asset-light production model using external grinding partners while keeping final mixing and quality control within the team.

Main channels:

- WhatsApp orders
- Instagram and Facebook awareness
- Meta ads
- Retail shelf placement
- Nutritionist referrals
- Bykea/InDrive delivery

## Traction

- First 50 kg production batch completed and sold
- Rs. 47,500 first-batch revenue generated
- Rs. 37,500 reinvested into the second production cycle
- 50 kg supplied to Sheikhupura superstores
- 40 kg prepared for Lahore market expansion
- Two nutritionists agreed to support the referral model
- Initial Meta ads test generated direct orders from Lahore
